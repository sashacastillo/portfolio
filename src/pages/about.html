About Sasha Castillo

My Story

I am a passionate learner and communicator. Having pursued Graphic Design and Web Development, I have spent recent years expanding my knowledge to UX Design.

I have a growing portfolio that showcases an active involvement in all stages of a user-centered design process. My top UX/UI Design strengths are the ability to facilitate meaningful feedback, and to address user needs with functional and intuitive design.

I have lived for over 10 years in the "Mountain States", where I enjoy hiking, reading, spending time with friends, and exploring new things I haven't done before.
