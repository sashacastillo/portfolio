What is User Experience (UX) Design?

"User experience (UX) design is the process designers use to create websites that provide meaningful and relevant experiences to users." - Interaction Design Foundation. "User experience encompasses all aspects of the end-user's interaction with the company, its services, and its products." - Nielsen Norman Group

My Services

Expert Review

Design expert review is a usability method where an expert in the field inspects your website or app design and layout and makes a conclusion on how compliant it is with common usability best practices.


Visual Design

Visual design is an artistic appeal of your website. It includes design elements like layout, typography, color, graphics, and white space. A designer chooses elements to improve customer experience.


User Research

Qualitative research to learn about your customer’s needs. It includes methods such as user interviews, usability testing, focus groups, card sorting, first-click testing, navigation testing, or surveys.


Site Building

I work with common no-code website builders (like Square Space, Wix, Weebly, Shopify, and more) to customize your website design to make it effective in increasing your customer base and conversion.


Design Strategy

I will guide your through a simple questionnaire to learn about your goals and needs for your website. Together we will create a measurable plan of action to achieve your user experience goals with your online presence.


Individualized Solutions

Each client is unique. Your customers also have specific needs that need to be supported and fulfilled when they visit your website. We will work to identify those needs and create an effective online experience.
